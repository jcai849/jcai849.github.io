---
title: A Revised Middle Class Understanding of the State
date: 2022-05-26
---

The eye of the state is focused on trivial matters, leading to dysfunction (1) in important matters.
Complaints are raised about the dysfunction, demanding greater powers (2) to attend to the important matters.
The state happily increases it's power, which it readily makes use of to stifle complaints (3), and focus further on trivialities.

Examples:

- \(1) Crime, (2) competent police, (3) enforcers
- \(1) Ignorance, (2) educators, (3) administrators
