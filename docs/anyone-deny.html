Anyone can Deny

Jason Cairns

2022-05-15

Prove the metaphysical by way of the physical? What would a proof even mean to you? Can you see, hear, taste “proof”? What can I point to, to show you “meaning”? Pessoa: “Only a materialist can utter such a phrase, because everyone who utters such a phrase is, whether he knows it or not, a materialist. What does he plan to take from life, and how? Where will he take his pork chops and red wine and lady friend? To what heaven that he doesn’t believe in?”
