Peculiarities of Managerialism

Jason Cairns

2022-05-10

Why does managerialism always exhibit such specific and often bizarre forms? Why do the individuals upholding it so often tend to eunuchs? – all the more curious, for the emperor no longer has any wives. Is positing the question akin to questioning a fundamental physical constant? Or in the fashion of mathematical proof, shall we consider if it were not the case, and lead ourselves to contradiction, so as to walk back and witness the necessity of it?
