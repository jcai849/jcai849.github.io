title: Emergency Conversation
date: 2022-06-09
---

After Finally Getting Through to the Correct Ward:

"I just pulled a girl off the railing of the Grafton Road bridge when she was about to jump. Apparently this is the second time she's done this while staying at your hospital; she just tried it yesterday. So you clearly don't have the proper measures in place. I'm very concerned and she needs to be in a more secure facility."

"So what was the question?"

"That was not a question, it was an instruction."

"Ok, thank you for calling."

"Wait! What? This girl is not safe, she is not getting proper care there."

"We can't disclose any information about patients."

"Is- is there some way I can keep track of this?"

"Are you an immediate family member?"

"No, I was just driving past and I saw this girl with her legs over the edge and-"

"Okay, well, because you're not an immediate family member, and because she's a minor, I can't give you any information about her. This is the emergency department and she's not in the emergency room any more."

I had nothing to say, and listened to the line go dead.
