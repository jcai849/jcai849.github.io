---
title: Adrift
date: 2022-05-10
---

I feel like I've had my king checkmated in this dream, but my demonic opponent has returned pieces to the board and let me keep playing without me realising.
I'm not sure at what point the mate occurred, but there's no return.

Where was the point of departure?
Impulsive thoughts provide clues;
Maybe I'll grow into this shoe.
I'll catch a bus to the park.
I'll remember that, to tell my dad.

Memories:
A song that I stopped enjoying years ago.
A feeling, not too different, many years ago: adrift.
