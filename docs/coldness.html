Coldness

Jason Cairns

2022-05-30

Coldness between people serves to reinforce itself. The only way to break it is to be the first one vulnerable. Take it on the chin if it doesn’t work out, and continue being vulnerable. Be honest. You do in fact care. Of course you care. If you truly don’t, find a way to – you have a duty to those whom you are bound to.

On the other hand, if a relationship is destructive, or on such a trajectory that can’t be stopped, then cut your ties. It’s never worth destroying yourself for such a relationship, and self-destruction always extends it’s malign influence outwards to your other relationships. Leave, but leave your heart open. Remain able to forgive. But until such a miracle as a request for forgiveness takes place, get the hell out of there, and don’t look back.
