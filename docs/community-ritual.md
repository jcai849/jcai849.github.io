*N.B. I think of this as [Valentine](https://profiles.auckland.ac.nz/val-ibeka) tells me of [months mind](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Month%27s_mind), something that was not to be mentioned otherwise -- grief, as a non-saccharine emotion, being important to hide.*

The rituals surrounding life's inevitable stages are worn down as much as denial of life stages exists in a culture.
Knowledge of these rituals declines commensurately with the decline of community.
Insofar as a society ignores this yet remains functioning, it also ignores that it only survives because of the residual community and their rituals.
As this disappears, further atomisation, alienation, and general social malaise grows.
The ultimate cause is largely ideological.
