---
title: How's That Working Out for You?
date: 2022-05-31
---

Just keep asking it.
Every grand explanation, every trivial complaint, it has to be asked.
Don't stop reassessing.
Purity spirals and a general loss of track of reality always manifest themselves in the absence of this question.
There is no grand clockmaker, the hourglass needs constant turning.

> TYLER
>
> How's that working out for you?
>
> JACK
>
> What?
>
> TYLER
>
> Being clever.
>
> JACK
>
> (thrown)
> Well, uh... great.
>
> TYLER
>
> Keep it up, then. Keep it right up.[@uhls1998]

As an aside, the screenplay is the correct answer to the question of whether the book or film was better.

> If the rule you followed brought you to this, of what use was the rule?
