---
title: Mann gegen Mann
date: 2022-05-13
---

Maximisation of man's power over self, in it's modern understanding, necessitates the crowding out of other men's power over him.
Such is liberation.
The breaking of bonds leads him to stand alone.
This power for oneself has created an isolated atom, and erases one half of the social animal.
Man ceases to have definition.
Singular man is as much a contradiction as is private language.
In practice, the relation to the state remains and grows all the stronger, with the residual social energies directed to this singular relationship.
Self power defeats itself.
