title: Some Story about Human Nature
date: 2022-04-04
---

- The erasure of aggression by those seeking peace.
- The accusation of erasure of human nature.
- The defence that it is specifically aggression, not human nature, being erased.
- The question of why one element of emotion, pacifism, be allowed such dominion over another, aggression.
- The continuation of the point that all emotions are required for a full human experience.
- The retort that war between the emotions is human nature, and that aggression is simply losing.
- The sequel, where aggression learns how to become itself.
