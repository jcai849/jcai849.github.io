date: 2022-04-04

- The erasure of wrath by those seeking peace.
- The accusation of erasure of human nature.
- The defence that it is specifically wrath, not human nature, being erased.
- The question of why one element of emotion, pacifism, be allowed such dominion over another.
- The continuation of the point that all emotions are required for a full human experience.
- The retort that war between the emotions is human nature, and that wrath is simply losing.
- The sequel, where wrath learns how to become itself.
